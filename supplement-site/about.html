The Ingredients

Turmeric Complex Extract Blend — 900mg

The powerhouse of our formula. Our 900mg Turmeric Complex combines turmeric root extract with curcuminoids standardized to 95% for maximum anti-inflammatory potency. Curcumin modulates multiple inflammatory pathways including NF-kB and COX-2. Paired with black pepper extract (piperine) to solve curcumin's notoriously poor bioavailability — because an ingredient you can't absorb is an ingredient you're wasting.

Collagen, Glucosamine, Chondroitin & Hyaluronic Acid

Four joint-building compounds working in concert. Hydrolyzed Collagen Type II (100mg) supports cartilage structure. Chondroitin Sulfate (100mg) rebuilds cartilage and supports flexibility. Glucosamine Sulfate (100mg) stimulates production of glycosaminoglycans — the building blocks of healthy cartilage. Hyaluronic Acid (100mg) lubricates joints and supports synovial fluid for smoother movement.

Boswellia, Ginger, MSM, Bromelain & More

Boswellia Serrata (80mg) provides anti-inflammatory support through a complementary pathway, targeting 5-LOX enzymes. Vitamin C (60mg) is essential for collagen synthesis. Ginger Root Extract (50mg) adds natural anti-inflammatory benefits. MSM (25mg) supports joint recovery and reduces oxidative stress. Bromelain (25mg at 2,500 GDU/g) is a powerful enzyme for tissue repair. Black Pepper Extract (5mg) ties it all together by enhancing nutrient absorption across the formula.
