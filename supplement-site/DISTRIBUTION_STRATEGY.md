# Laibar Wellness — Distribution Strategy

## Overview
Go-to-market distribution plan for Laibar Joint Support, a premium multi-action joint supplement with 12 active ingredients. Target: active adults 30-65 who are proactive about joint health and willing to pay a premium for transparent, high-quality formulas.

---

## Phase 1: Direct-to-Consumer Foundation (Months 1-3)

### Website (laibarwellness.com)
- **Primary sales channel** — Netlify-hosted site with Stripe checkout
- One-time purchase ($64.99) and Subscribe & Save ($57.19/mo, 12% off)
- Free shipping on all orders
- 60-day money-back guarantee to reduce purchase friction
- Email capture (10% first order discount) to build owned audience

### Amazon FBA
- Launch on Amazon with Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) for Prime eligibility
- Create A+ Content with supplement facts, ingredient breakdowns, lifestyle imagery
- Target keywords: "turmeric joint supplement," "glucosamine chondroitin supplement," "natural joint pain relief"
- Vine program for initial reviews (30-50 units)
- Sponsored Products campaigns targeting competitor brands and category keywords
- **Goal**: 50+ verified reviews within 60 days

### Email Marketing
- Welcome series (5 emails): brand story, ingredient science, usage tips, social proof, discount reminder
- Weekly newsletter: joint health tips, new blog content, subscriber-only offers
- Cart abandonment sequence (3 emails)
- Re-order reminders at day 25 (before 30-day supply runs out)
- Platform: Klaviyo or Mailchimp

---

## Phase 2: Social & Content Marketing (Months 2-6)

### Instagram
- Brand aesthetic: clean, dark + gold, premium feel
- Content pillars: ingredient education, customer results, behind-the-scenes, lifestyle
- Reels: "What's actually in your joint supplement?" comparison series
- Stories: daily polls, Q&As, customer testimonial highlights
- Hashtag strategy: #jointhealth #turmeric #supplementtransparency #activeover40

### TikTok
- Short-form educational content: "Reading supplement labels," "5 red flags in joint supplements"
- Partner with fitness creators 35+ who deal with joint issues
- "Day in my life" content featuring Laibar as part of morning routine

### YouTube
- Long-form ingredient breakdowns (SEO play for "turmeric curcumin benefits," "glucosamine vs chondroitin")
- Customer journey/testimonial videos
- Partner with health/fitness YouTubers for honest reviews

### Blog / SEO
- Already live: 2 articles + 4 planned
- Target 2 new articles per month focused on long-tail keywords
- Topics: ingredient research, joint health for specific activities (running, yoga, CrossFit, golf), comparison content
- Internal linking strategy to product page

### Podcast Advertising
- Target health, fitness, and wellness podcasts with 35+ audience
- Host-read ads perform best — provide talking points + promo codes
- Podcasts to target: Huberman Lab, Mind Pump, The Model Health Show, Wellness Mama

---

## Phase 3: Retail & Wholesale (Months 6-12)

### Online Marketplaces
- **Walmart Marketplace** — growing supplement category, less competition than Amazon
- **iHerb** — international reach, health-focused audience
- **Vitacost / Thrive Market** — premium health consumer base

### Specialty Retail (Local/Regional First)
- Independent health food stores and supplement shops
- Pitch: "Premium formula, transparent labeling, strong margins"
- Wholesale pricing: 50% off MSRP for retailers (wholesale $32.50, retail $64.99)
- Provide POP displays, shelf talkers, sample sachets

### Gym & Studio Partnerships
- Partner with CrossFit boxes, yoga studios, physical therapy clinics
- Consignment or wholesale model
- Co-branded content: "Recommended by [Studio Name]"
- Offer affiliate commission (15-20%) for referrals via unique codes

### Practitioner Channel
- Chiropractors, physical therapists, sports medicine practitioners
- Professional pricing (40% off MSRP)
- Provide clinical ingredient data sheets
- "Recommended by professionals" trust signal for website

---

## Phase 4: Scale & Expand (Months 12-24)

### Influencer & Affiliate Program
- Tiered affiliate structure:
  - Micro (1K-10K followers): 15% commission + free product
  - Mid (10K-100K): 20% commission + free product + content budget
  - Macro (100K+): custom deals, potential equity/advisory
- Platform: ShareASale, Impact, or Refersion
- Focus on fitness, health, wellness, and active lifestyle niches

### Subscription Box Partnerships
- Partner with wellness subscription boxes for sampling
- Targets: FabFitFun, Bulu Box, Vitacrate
- Goal: drive trial and convert to direct subscribers

### International Expansion
- Start with Canada, UK, Australia (English-speaking markets)
- Amazon UK/CA/AU as initial channels
- Ensure regulatory compliance (Health Canada NPN, UK FSA)

### Retail Chain Pitch
- Whole Foods (regional buyer meetings)
- Sprouts Farmers Market
- GNC / Vitamin Shoppe
- Requires: established sales velocity, UPC codes, EDI capability, liability insurance

## Key Metrics to Track

- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Target < $25 for DTC
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: Target $200+ (4+ orders average)
- **Subscription conversion rate**: Target 30% of customers on Subscribe & Save
- **Amazon BSR**: Target top 100 in Joint Health Supplements within 6 months
- **Reorder rate**: Target 40%+ within 60 days
- **Website conversion rate**: Target 3-4% from organic traffic

## Competitive Differentiation

1. **12 active ingredients** — most competitors use 3-5
2. **900mg turmeric complex** — industry-leading dose with 95% curcuminoids
3. **Full transparency** — no proprietary blends, exact doses on label
4. **Premium branding** — elevated aesthetic vs. clinical/generic supplement look
5. **Black pepper for absorption** — addresses bioavailability gap most brands ignore
6. **Multi-pathway approach** — targets inflammation, cartilage repair, lubrication, and recovery simultaneously
