The Ingredients

Glucosamine Sulfate — 1500mg

The gold standard for joint support. Glucosamine sulfate is a naturally occurring compound that stimulates the production of glycosaminoglycans and proteoglycans — the building blocks of healthy cartilage. At 1500mg, our dose matches the levels used in major clinical trials showing significant improvements in joint comfort and function.

Turmeric Extract — 1000mg (95% Curcuminoids)

Curcumin is one of nature's most potent anti-inflammatory compounds, modulating multiple pathways including NF-kB and COX-2. Our extract is standardized to 95% curcuminoids for maximum potency. Paired with BioPerine® to solve curcumin's notoriously poor bioavailability — because an ingredient you can't absorb is an ingredient you're wasting.

Boswellia, Type II Collagen & BioPerine®

Boswellia serrata (600mg) provides anti-inflammatory support through a complementary pathway to curcumin, targeting 5-LOX enzymes. Type II Collagen (500mg) supports cartilage structure and joint cushioning. BioPerine® (10mg) — a patented black pepper extract — ties it all together by dramatically enhancing nutrient absorption.
